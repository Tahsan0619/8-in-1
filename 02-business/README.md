# Business / Corporate Demo

A polished corporate website blending the visual authority of **Stripe**, the spatial elegance of **Linear**, and the narrative depth of **McKinsey** — with the warm approachability of **Mailchimp's** brand voice.

## Tech Approach
- HTML5 / CSS3 (custom properties, grid, flexbox)
- Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+ modules)
- Intersection Observer for scroll animations
- CSS scroll-snap for certain sections
- No frameworks

## Design Philosophy
- **Authority through restraint** — sophisticated type, ample whitespace
- **Story-driven** — every section has a narrative arc
- **Scroll-activated motion** — elements reveal as you scroll
- **Photography-forward** — real team photos, case study visuals
- **Conversion-focused** — strategic CTA placement

## Key Differentiators
- Smooth scroll-triggered animations (not jarring AOS defaults)
- Company story as an interactive timeline
- Case studies with problem→solution→result narrative
- Pricing toggle (monthly/yearly) with smooth transition
- Contact form with budget range slider
- Blog with estimated reading time
